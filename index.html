ROBLOX INK GAME LIGHTS OUT WEAPONS

Item Description

The Fork item is obtainable by going to the right guard offering Kimbap before the start of Lights Out, or selecting it during Last Dinner. It will allow you to attack other players during both. The Fork will deal 18 damage per M1 during Lights Out, but will deal 25 damage per M1 during Last Dinner. Damage is consistent, and hitting a player twice will cause them to ragdoll. A lunge attack can be performed with this weapon, however will deal the same amount as an M1, with the benefits of having momentum, but the downside of being easily punished. The Fork has a larger hitbox than the Bottle.

Obtainability

This item is only obtainable in the Lights Out game mode after picking the right table before Lights Out. It can also be obtainable during the Last Dinnerif a player selects it.

Item description

The Bottle item is obtainable by going to the left guard offering Onigiri and Soda together before the start of Lights Out, or by selecting it during Last Dinner. It will allow you to attack other players during both. The Bottle deals 30 damage upon shattering it over a players head, and will deal 15 damage per M1 after. During Last Dinner, it will deal 40 damage upon shattering it over a players head, and will deal 20 damage per M1 after. Hitting a player twice will cause them to ragdoll. A lunge attack can be performed with this weapon, however will deal the same amount as an M1, with the benefits of having momentum, but the downside of being easily punished. The Bottle has a smaller hitbox than Fork.

Obtainability

is item is only obtainable in the Lights Out game mode after picking the left table before lights out. It can also be obtainable during the Last Dinner if a player selects it.
